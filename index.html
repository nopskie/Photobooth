About Photogenic ✨

Welcome to Photogenic! This is a fun, interactive web-based photobooth experience designed to let you capture your favorite moments directly from your browser.

We provide a variety of aesthetic frames, ranging from vintage polaroids to cute themed layouts, along with powerful pixel-magic filters like Dot Screen, Grit, and Distort to make your photos truly stand out.

Simply choose your layout, set your timer, strike a pose, and save your beautiful memories!

Made with ❤️ by nopskie.

Privacy Policy 🔒

Your privacy is our top priority. We want you to feel completely safe while using Photogenic.

1. Local Processing Only: All photo capturing, filtering, and processing happen entirely within your own web browser.

2. No Cloud Storage: We do NOT upload, save, or transmit your camera feed or captured photos to any external servers or databases. Once you close this tab, your photos are gone forever unless you manually downloaded them.

3. Camera Access: Camera permissions are strictly used to display the live preview and capture the photos for your photostrip during your active session.

Enjoy capturing moments with peace of mind!
